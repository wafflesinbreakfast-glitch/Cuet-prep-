          Score maximisation tips
          NCERT verbatim reading
          CUET questions are lifted almost word-for-word from NCERT. Read the text — don't just read coaching notes.
          Negative marking strategy
          With −1 per wrong, skip questions you're less than 60% sure about. Target 40–42 accurate attempts over 50 rushed ones.
          History: source extracts
          Practice identifying which theme a passage belongs to. CUET regularly gives source quotes to match to context.
          Geography: maps matter
          Mark major industrial regions, population clusters, and transport routes on a blank India map. Easy guaranteed marks.
          English: speed reading
          Practice skimming passages in 90 seconds. You have ~72 seconds per question — RC passages eat time.
          PYQ papers are gold
          Solve CUET 2022, 2023, 2024 papers per subject. Same themes, data points, extracts repeat across years.
